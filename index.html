Hi, I’m Melanie 👋

I’m a Master's graduate in Mathematics in Data Science at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), with a focus on machine learning, statistics, and computer vision. I enjoy building methods that are both mathematically sound and useful in practice.

My main research interest is AI for healthcare — especially working with physiological signals (e.g., ECG) and bridging the gap between prototypes and real-world deployment. I’m also excited about robust evaluation, generalization, and “what breaks when things get messy”.

Outside of research, I love hands-on projects — for example, I’m building PoseQuest, a computer-vision based coaching tool for equestrian sports.
